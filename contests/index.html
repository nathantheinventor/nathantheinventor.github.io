Programming Contests

I've been privileged to participate in a number of programming contests over the last few years. For the first contest, one of my professors invited me to go to the contest, but I was fairly reluctant. But my professor pushed me to go, and I have never regretted it. Over the years, I've also solved a number of problems on sites such as UVa, Kattis, HackerRank, CodeForces, and others. You can find my solutions to many of the problems I've solved here.
